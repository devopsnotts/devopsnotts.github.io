About

DevOps Notts is a meetup for those interested or working in DevOps and cloud native technology, culture, practices and tooling. Our goal is to help make Nottingham the DevOps capital of the UK!

As a group, we strive to build a community of DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering fans, innovators, implementors and experts from across a wide spectrum of people - maybe they're a C-level executive, a junior developer or maybe they just have a passion for technology. The DevOps Notts community includes everyone and anyone - you are always welcome here!
